Science and Technical Writing

Science and Technical Writing (STW) at Mass Academy is taught by Ms. Curran. The class focuses on reading and writing scientific pieces in order to prepare us for writing our own scientific research/experimental paper for our STEM projects.

We focus on concisely summing up articles, like this summary I wrote about women in the math field. We also wrote a grant proposal for an EngineerGirl essay contest. The paper was about an endangered animal, and we had to come up with engineering solutions for helping the animal. I chose the Vaquita Porpoise.

Towards the end of the year, we started focusing on college writing, and drafted our first collee essays.
