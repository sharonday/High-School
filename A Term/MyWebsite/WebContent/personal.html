MY NAME IS...

Hi! My name is Daya Sharon and I am currently a junior at the Massachusetts Academy of Math and Science (also known as Mass Academy).
Mass Academy is a math and science based high school for juniors and seniors. Before Mass Academy, I went to Westborough High School.

My hobbies include swimming, rereading Harry Potter, and creating things.
I used to swim competitively but am currently taking a break to adjust to the more intense school environment of Mass Academy.

I was born in Jerusalem, Israel in November 1999. My family consists of my mom, my dad, me, and my two younger sisters. We have an almost five-year-old cat named Gingy. Gingy is the Hebrew word for ginger and my cat is definitely a red-head.

This past summer, I went to Israel with a leadership program from my camp. We traveled the country up and down. Those long bus rides made for great naps. Part of the program was coming back to camp for two weeks after five weeks in Israel. At camp, we were considered "CITs", or counselors-in-training. We learned how to be counselors and we had lots of fun throughout the entire time. Here's a picture of me at the top of Masada., watching the sunrise.

For my community service junior year, I was an assistant teacher at Beth Tikvah Synagogue Religious School. I helped teach Hebrew language, reading and writing, to first and second grade students.

I was also a part of the E-NABLE group at Mass Academy. We 3D printed prosthetic hands for people in need. The process included stringing hands together and using hot glue, screws, and velcro.
